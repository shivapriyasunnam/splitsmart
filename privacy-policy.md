# Privacy Policy for SplitSmart

**Last Updated:** May 30, 2026

## Introduction

SplitSmart ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information in our mobile application.

---

## Data Collection and Storage

**We do NOT collect, transmit, or store any of your personal data on external servers.**

All data created and managed within SplitSmart is stored **locally on your device only**, including:

- Expense records and split details
- Member names and balances
- Budget and category information
- Sync history and settings

---

## Data We Do NOT Collect

We do not collect, access, or share:

- Your name, email address, or any personal identifiers
- Financial account information
- Location data
- Device identifiers or advertising IDs
- Analytics or usage data

---

## Google Drive Sync (Optional)

If you choose to use the Google Drive sync feature:

- You authorize SplitSmart to access your Google Drive solely to read and write files within a dedicated `SplitSmart/` folder
- **All data is encrypted on your device using AES-256 before being uploaded** — we cannot read your data, and neither can Google
- The encryption passphrase never leaves your device
- We do not access any other files in your Google Drive
- You can revoke Drive access at any time via your Google account settings

Google Sign-In is used only to obtain an OAuth token for Drive access. We do not use your Google account for any other purpose.

---

## Bluetooth Sync (Optional)

If you use Bluetooth sync:

- Data is transferred directly between your two devices over a local Bluetooth connection
- All transferred data is encrypted before transmission
- No data passes through any server or third party

---

## Backups

Backup files exported from SplitSmart are encrypted and saved to a location you choose on your device. We do not have access to these files.

---

## Third-Party Services

SplitSmart does not integrate with any analytics, advertising, or crash-reporting services.

---

## Children's Privacy

SplitSmart is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect any data from children.
